Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Suger
  • Maida
  • Oil
  • Milk

Recipe

  1. Roll the dough, cool it slightly, then cut into diamond shapes.
  2. Shape into smooth balls without cracks.
  3. Deep fry onlow flame until golden brwon.
  4. Soak in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom.

Kaju Barfi

Ingredients

  • Cashew nuts
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Boil sugar with water to make 1-string syrup.
  3. Add cashew powder and cook until a soft dough forms.
  4. Roll the dough, cool it slightly, then cut into diamond shapes

Maggi

Ingredients

  • Maggi noddles
  • Masala
  • Butter
  • Veggies

Recipe

  1. Boil 1/2 half cup of water.
  2. Add Noodles and masla.
  3. Cook 2-3 min, stirring until water is obsorbed
  4. Add Veggies while stirring and butter at the end.

Ingredients

Recipe
